About Us

Recruit & Retain (R&R) is the #1 provider of workforce analytics solutions. We are committed to helping your organization use human resource data to make informed personnel decisions. Many driving factors contribute to employee retention; we help you identify the employee lifecycle element that has the greatest impact on your ability to recruit and retain a high-performing workforce. Let us assist you with recruiting, onboarding, developing, and retaining a thriving workforce.

Staffing and Recruitment

We can help you place your job postings in the right job advertising networks to increase your possibility of obtaining the personnel to help you thrive in the 21st-century business environment.

Cost Analysis

We help clients realize the cost of their human capital needs. Attacking, onboarding, developing, and retaining employees requires a significant about of effort and time, impacting your bottom line.

Turnover Minimization

Hiring requires a great deal of effort; retaining great talent is essential to the company's longevity. We help organizations develop work benefits packages that help minimize turnover and help stabilize and recruit high performing workforce..

Workforce Demographics

Understanding your workforce's demographics against those of your competitors will increase your ability to compete for high performers' services. Let us assist you with understanding your workforce profile.

Workforce Forecasting

We provide the basis for efficient, agile workforce scheduling. We help you predict which employees with what qualifications are to be deployed where, when, and at what cost.

Employee Engagement

An engaged workforce is a thriving collaborative environment that allows for creativity and innovation to run freely. We help our clients create engaging environments that attract the best talent.
